About Us

Ayushar is India’s first and only digital platform dedicated to empowering Ayurvedic practitioners with modern digital tools—bringing ancient solutions to modern lives. We aim to revive and promote Ayurveda by supporting practitioners and offering people a healthier lifestyle with minimal or no side effects.

How Ayushar Works?

At Ayushar, authenticity and trust are our top priorities.

We personally verify each Ayurvedic doctor listed on our platform by checking their practice, reviewing patient records, and validating their medical certifications—eliminating the chances of scammers.

We also verify Ayurvedic products by conducting independent lab tests to ensure that all health claims are truthful and not exaggerated.
